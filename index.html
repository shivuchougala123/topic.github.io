How it's addressed:Highlights gaps in candidates' skills, enabling the development of tailored training programs and education systems to address these deficiencies. It ensures inclusive and equitable access to education.

How it's addressed:By ensuring fair access to training and employment opportunities, skill gap analysis promotes social inclusion, reduces inequality, and contributes to building transparent and just institutions.
